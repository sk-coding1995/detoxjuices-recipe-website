About us

DetoxJuices was created to help people discover healthy, delicious juices that support natural detoxing. Our goal is to keep it simple and enjoyable using nutritious ingredients that you can find at a very afforable price at any supermarket.

We believe that incorporating fresh juices into your diet can have numerous health benefits. Fresh juices can be a convenient way to increase fruit and venetable intake. Our team is passionate about sharing the joy of juicing and providing resources for a healthier lifestyle.

What we will provide

  1. Juice recipes using everyday ingredients.
  2. Information on the health benefits of the recipes.
  3. Regular updates with new juice ideas.
  4. A layout that stays readable on smartphone devices.

How This Website Helps Users

  • As a busy person I want quick on-the-go juice ideas so I can make something healthy very easily.
  • As someone with poor dietary habits I want nutritious juice recipes so I can improve my diet over time.
  • As a smartphone user I want the pages to stay readable and work well on my device.

Accessibility approach

We have used colours that are easy on the eyes with good contrast ratios, keyboard-friendly navigation and descriptive alt text for images to ensure that all users, including those with disabilities, can access and enjoy our content.
