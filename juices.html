Juice recipes

Pick something healthy and delicious from our small selection of juice recipes. You can juice, or blend if you don't have a juicer.

Two recipes. Backed by nutritionists. Change is today.

Green Refresh

  • 1 cucumber
  • 2 celery stalks
  • 1 green apple
  • 1 handful spinach
  • 1/2 lemon, peeled
  • 1 teaspoon spirulina powder

Suggestion: If it tastes too bitter, add only quarter tea spoon of spirulina.

Betroot Glow

  • 1 medium beetroot
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 orange (peeled)
  • 1/2 lemon, peeled

Suggestion: Add a pinch of celtic salt to absorb more minerals.
